Maritime Silk Road

From the ancient ports of the Qin and Han dynasties to the modern cruise home port, traverse 2,000 years of history and visit landmarks such as Nanhai God Temple, Huangpu Ancient Port, and Thirteen Hongs that witnessed the exchange of Chinese and foreign civilizations.
