Guangzhou Innovation Culture Theme Tour

As a frontier of China's reform and opening-up, Guangzhou has nurtured rich innovative achievements in institutional reform, urban construction, and cultural inheritance. This one-day route will take you through Guangzhou's innovative footprint from the early reform era to the present, showcasing the city's vitality and creativity.

Featured Attractions

A one-day journey covering institutional breakthroughs, industrial transformation, urban construction, and cultural inheritance. Click below to explore detailed itineraries.

Innovation Landmarks

· White Swan Hotel: China's first Sino-foreign cooperative five-star hotel, pioneering "open lobby" design and public access
· Canton Tower: Modern landmark with "slim waist" aesthetics, solving wind resistance challenges with global's highest Ferris wheel
· Haixin Bridge: World's widest curved-beam arch bridge, using 3D imaging and BIM for millimeter-level precision

Innovation Culture Itinerary

· Morning: Institutional & Industrial Innovation: White Swan Hotel → "Pioneer" Statue (Development Zone)
· Afternoon: Urban Construction Innovation: Tianhe CBD Twin Towers → Canton Tower → Haixin Bridge
· Evening: Cultural Heritage Innovation: Yongqing Fang → Beijing Road → Guangzhou Higher Education Mega Center

Innovation Highlights

· 1983: White Swan Hotel opens as China's first Sino-foreign five-star hotel, breaking elitist service norms
· 1984: Guangzhou Economic and Technological Development Zone established, "Pioneer" statue symbolizes tech-industry integration
· 2016-2022: Yongqing Fang's "micro-renovation" revives arcades, merging tradition with modern commerce
· 2021: Haixin Bridge opens as smart pedestrian bridge, using 5G and BIM technology for safety and experience
